Description

  • A lightweight OWL 2 DL ontology modeling the energy news media landscape as observed through Bluesky social media posts.

  • SKOS topic individuals for the Energy News Ontology concept scheme.

  • A SKOS concept scheme organizing energy topic individuals into a broader/narrower hierarchy for classifying energy news articles.
